# Forge Fitness CRM Email Portfolio

A professional CRM and HTML email development portfolio project created for a fictional commercial gym brand called **Forge Fitness**.

This project simulates the customer communication system of a modern fitness company, including responsive email templates, customer lifecycle journeys, CRM logic, personalisation examples, and marketing automation concepts.

## Project Goal

The goal of this portfolio is to demonstrate practical skills in HTML email development, CRM email strategy, responsive design, and customer lifecycle communication.

This project is designed to reflect the type of work used by gyms, subscription businesses, e-commerce companies, fintech companies, and digital platforms that communicate with customers through email campaigns and automated CRM journeys.

## Brand Scenario

**Forge Fitness** is a fictional commercial gym company based in London.

The company offers:

* Monthly gym memberships
* Personal training sessions
* Fitness classes
* Member referrals
* Renewal reminders
* Inactive member reactivation campaigns

## Emails Included

This portfolio will include:

1. Welcome Email
2. Membership Confirmation Email
3. Class Booking Confirmation Email
4. Membership Renewal Reminder
5. Failed Payment Email
6. Personal Training Promotion Email
7. Win-Back Email
8. Referral Campaign Email

## CRM Journeys Included

The project will also include customer journey examples such as:

* New member onboarding journey
* Membership renewal journey
* Failed payment recovery journey
* Inactive member win-back journey
* Referral journey
