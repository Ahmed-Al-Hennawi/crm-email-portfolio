# Class Booking Confirmation Email

## Purpose

This email confirms that a Forge Fitness member has successfully booked a class.

## Trigger

The email is sent when a member books a class through the member area or gym app.

## Audience

Active gym members who have booked a studio class.

## Personalisation Fields

- first_name
- class_name
- class_date
- class_time
- home_gym
- instructor_name
- cancellation_window

## Goal

The goal of this email is to confirm the booking, provide class details, reduce confusion before arrival, and encourage the member to manage their booking through the member area.

## CRM Logic

1. Member opens the class timetable.
2. Member selects a class.
3. Booking is confirmed.
4. Confirmation email is sent.
5. Member can view, cancel, or manage the booking.

## Email Type

Transactional / CRM email.
