# Membership Confirmation Email

## Purpose

This email confirms that a new Forge Fitness membership has been successfully created and activated.

## Trigger

The email is sent after a customer completes registration and payment.

## Audience

New paying gym members.

## Personalisation Fields

- first_name
- last_name
- membership_type
- home_gym
- start_date
- monthly_price

## Goal

The goal of this email is to confirm the membership, provide key access details, and guide the customer toward their first visit.

## CRM Logic

1. Customer completes signup.
2. Payment is successful.
3. Membership record is created.
4. Confirmation email is sent.
5. Customer is directed to the member area.

## Email Type

Transactional / CRM email.
