# Personal Training Promotion Email

## Purpose

This email promotes personal training consultations to Forge Fitness members.

## Trigger

The email is sent to members who may benefit from extra support, such as new members, inactive members, or members who have not yet booked a personal training session.

## Audience

Forge Fitness members who are eligible for a personal training offer.

## Personalisation Fields

- first_name
- start_date
- home_gym
- offer_name
- offer_expiry
- training_focus
- suggested_session

## Goal

The goal of this email is to increase personal training bookings, support new or returning members, and help members build a structured training routine.

## CRM Logic

1. CRM checks member profile and activity.
2. Member is eligible for a personal training offer.
3. CRM selects a suggested training focus or session type.
4. Personal training promotion email is sent.
5. Member is directed to book a consultation.

## Email Type

Upsell CRM / promotional lifecycle email.
