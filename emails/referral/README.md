# Referral Campaign Email

## Purpose

This email encourages Forge Fitness members to invite friends to join the gym through a referral reward campaign.

## Trigger

The email is sent to eligible members who have an active membership and meet the referral campaign criteria.

## Audience

Active Forge Fitness members who may be likely to refer friends, based on membership status, visit activity, or engagement.

## Personalisation Fields

- first_name
- home_gym
- referral_code
- reward_amount
- friend_reward
- offer_expiry

## Goal

The goal of this email is to drive new member acquisition through existing members, increase referrals, and reward loyal customers.

## CRM Logic

1. CRM checks active members.
2. Member is eligible for a referral campaign.
3. A unique referral code is generated or retrieved.
4. Referral email is sent to the member.
5. Member shares code with a friend.
6. Reward is applied after the friend joins and completes payment.

## Email Type

Growth CRM / referral campaign email.
