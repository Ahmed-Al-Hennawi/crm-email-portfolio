# Membership Renewal Reminder Email

## Purpose

This email reminds Forge Fitness members that their membership is due to renew soon.

## Trigger

The email is sent automatically before the member's renewal date.

## Audience

Active members with an upcoming membership renewal.

## Personalisation Fields

- first_name
- membership_type
- home_gym
- renewal_date
- renewal_amount
- payment_method

## Goal

The goal of this email is to reduce failed renewals, remind members about upcoming payments, and encourage continued gym engagement.

## CRM Logic

1. CRM checks active memberships.
2. Member has a renewal date within the reminder window.
3. Email is sent before renewal.
4. Member can review payment details or manage their membership.
5. Member is encouraged to book their next session.

## Email Type

Retention / subscription lifecycle CRM email.
