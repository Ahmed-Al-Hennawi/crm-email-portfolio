# Welcome Email

## Purpose

This email is sent immediately after a new member joins Forge Fitness.

## Trigger

A new customer completes membership registration and payment.

## Audience

New gym members.

## Personalisation Fields

- first_name
- membership_type
- home_gym
- start_date

## Goal

The goal of this email is to welcome the member, confirm their membership, and encourage them to book their first class.

## CRM Logic

1. User signs up.
2. Payment is successful.
3. Membership becomes active.
4. Welcome email is sent.
5. User is encouraged to book their first class.

## Email Type

Lifecycle / transactional CRM email.
