# Win-Back Campaign Email

## Purpose

This email encourages inactive Forge Fitness members to return to the gym and restart their training routine.

## Trigger

The email is sent when a member has not visited the gym for a defined inactivity period.

## Audience

Members who have not checked in recently but still have an active or recently inactive membership.

## Personalisation Fields

- first_name
- last_visit_date
- home_gym
- recommended_class
- offer_code
- offer_expiry

## Goal

The goal of this email is to reactivate inactive members, encourage class bookings, and reduce membership cancellations caused by low engagement.

## CRM Logic

1. CRM checks member visit activity.
2. Member has not visited within the inactivity window.
3. CRM selects a recommended class based on member activity or gym location.
4. Win-back email is sent with a return offer.
5. Member is directed to book a class or contact the team.

## Email Type

Marketing CRM / retention campaign email.
